Welcome to Maze using Angular and TensorFlow.js!

This game demonstrates the power of combining Angular and TensorFlow.js to build highly interactive and reactive applications. To play, we need access to your camera as your hand is the controller!

How to Play

To manage direction, use only 1-hand (preferablly your right) and a single digit (thumb or index finger recommended) to point in the direction that you want to go.
